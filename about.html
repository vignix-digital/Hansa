Who We Are?

Hansa is a leading exporter of premium-quality wool sourced directly from sheep and alpacas raised in the pristine mountains of Peru. Our products are renowned for their exceptional softness, durability, and eco-friendly production methods. We work closely with local farmers to ensure ethical treatment of animals and sustainable practices throughout the supply chain. Whether you seek luxurious alpaca yarns or rugged sheep wool, Hansa delivers only the finest fibers to designers and manufacturers worldwide.
