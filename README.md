# Hansa - Premium Wool & Alpaca Fiber Exports

**Hansa** is a leading exporter of premium-quality wool and alpaca fiber, sourced directly from the pristine mountains of Peru. Our website showcases our finest products with a modern, responsive ecommerce platform built using HTML and CSS.

## About Hansa

We specialize in exporting ethically-sourced, high-quality wool from sheep and alpacas raised in the Peruvian Andes. Our commitment to sustainability and animal welfare ensures that every product meets the highest standards. From luxurious alpaca blankets and sweaters to premium sheep wool rugs and throws, Hansa delivers only the finest fibers to designers and manufacturers worldwide.

**Location:** 369 Ludlow Ave, Cincinnati, OH 45220  
**Contact:** (513) 221-4002

## Features

- **Home:** Discover our featured products and learn about Hansa's commitment to premium quality and ethical practices.

- **Shop:** Browse our complete collection of alpaca and sheep wool products, including blankets, throws, rugs, scarves, and more.

- **About:** Learn about Hansa's story, our sourcing practices, and our commitment to supporting Peruvian farmers and sustainable production.

- **Contact:** Reach out to our team with inquiries, orders, or any questions about our products and services.

- **Blog:** Stay updated with articles about wool care, product highlights, and news from our Peruvian partners.

## Getting Started

1. Clone this repository to your local machine.

```bash
git clone https://github.com/Vannala-Rachana/hansa.git
```

2. Navigate to the project directory.

```bash
cd hansa
```

3. Open the `index.html` file in your preferred web browser to view the homepage.

4. Explore the shop, about, contact, and blog pages by clicking on the navigation links.
